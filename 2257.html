Verification of performers

Before any creator is approved to appear on this platform, we require:

  • A valid, unexpired government-issued photo identification document establishing that the performer is at least 18 years of age.
  • Confirmation of legal name, any professional or stage names used, and date of birth.
  • A signed consent and content release covering the material to be published.

Applications that do not satisfy these requirements are refused. Verification records are retained for the period required by law.

Content produced by third parties

For content licensed from third-party producers, the original producer retains responsibility as the custodian of records for that content. Juice Bunny requires written confirmation of 2257 compliance from every licensor before publication.

Zero tolerance policy

Juice Bunny has a zero-tolerance policy towards any content depicting minors, non-consensual activity, or any other illegal material. Any account found uploading, requesting or distributing such material is terminated immediately, and the matter is reported to the appropriate law-enforcement authorities.

To report suspected illegal content, email support@juicebunnytv.com with the URL and details. Reports are treated as urgent.

Age restriction

Access to this website is restricted to adults aged 18 or over, or the age of majority in the viewer's jurisdiction if higher. Parents and guardians can restrict access to adult material using parental control software.
